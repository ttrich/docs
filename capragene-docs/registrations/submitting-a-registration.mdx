---
title: Submitting a Registry Registration
description: How to submit an animal for registration with a breed registry.
---

## Before you submit — Readiness Check

When you open the registration dialog, a **Readiness Check** panel shows any issues with your animal's record before you submit:

| Severity | Issue | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| Critical | Missing microchip | High duplicate-detection risk — add a microchip number first |
| Critical | Short microchip | Microchip number appears too short to be valid |
| Critical | Missing date of birth | Required for registration |
| Warning | No sire linked | Parents not set — may affect DNA tier or be required by registry |
| Warning | No dam linked | Parents not set |
| Warning | Existing registration number | Animal may already be registered elsewhere |

Critical issues should be resolved before submitting. Warnings are informational — you can still submit, but they indicate potential issues the registrar may query.

## Membership requirement

Some registries require breeders to hold an active membership before submitting:

- A **green tick** confirms you are a member and can proceed.
- An **amber warning** means you are not a member. The Submit button will be disabled. Click the link to go to the **Membership Hub** and join.

## Submitting a registration

1. Open the animal's profile.
2. Click **Register with a Registry** or go to the **Registrations** tab.
3. Select the registry you want to register with from the list of available registries.
4. Review the readiness check and address any critical issues.
5. If a fee applies, complete payment via the payment step.
6. Click **Submit Application**.

Your application will appear as **Pending** while the registrar reviews it.

## Tracking your application — My Registrations

1. From the sidebar, click **My Registrations**.
2. All your registration submissions appear here with their current status:

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Pending | Awaiting registrar review |
| DNA Required | The registrar has requested a DNA test |
| DNA Submitted | Your DNA document has been uploaded and is under review |
| DNA Verified | DNA test has passed |
| Approved | Your animal is registered and has a registration number |
| Rejected | The application was declined (a reason will be provided) |

## Viewing your registration certificate

Once an application is **Approved**:

1. Go to **My Registrations**.
2. Find the approved registration.
3. Click **View Certificate**.
4. A printable certificate opens showing the animal's details, registration number, pedigree, and DNA verification status.
