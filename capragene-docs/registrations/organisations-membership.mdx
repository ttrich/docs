## The Membership Hub

The Membership Hub at **My Organisations** shows all open organisations you can join:

1. From the sidebar, click **My Organisations**.
2. Browse organisations that accept open membership.
3. Each listing shows the organisation name, description, website, annual fee, and the registries it manages.
4. Click **Join** to become a member.

## Viewing your memberships

The **My Organisations** page also shows all organisations you are already a member of, your role within each, and when your membership was last updated.

## Why membership matters

Some registries require breeders to hold an active membership with the managing organisation before they can submit animals for registration. If a registry has this requirement, it will be clearly shown in the membership check when you attempt to submit a registration — you will see an amber blocker with a link to join the relevant organisation.
