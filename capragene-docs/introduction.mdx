CapraGene is a goat management platform built for breeders who take their records seriously. Whether you run a small hobby herd or a professional stud, CapraGene gives you the tools to manage your animals, track bloodlines, submit for registry registration, and exhibit at shows — all in one place.

## What you can do with CapraGene

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Animal Records" icon="clipboard-list">
    Manage detailed profiles for every animal in your herd — health, breeding, production, and genetics all in one place.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Pedigree Registry" icon="sitemap">
    Submit animals for official registration with DNA-verified pedigrees and printable certificates.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Health & Breeding" icon="heart-pulse">
    Record health events, set reminders, track matings, pregnancies, and offspring.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Shows" icon="trophy">
    Enter animals in shows run through CapraGene and view your competition results.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
