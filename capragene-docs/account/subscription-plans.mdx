## Plans

CapraGene offers three subscription tiers:

| Plan | Who it is for |
|---|---|
| **Starter** (Free) | Hobby breeders wanting to manage a small herd and view pedigrees |
| **Breeder** | Active breeders needing registry submissions, health records, and show entries |
| **Stud Pro** | Professional studs requiring genetics tools, AI/semen inventory, and advanced production records |

## Upgrading your plan

1. From the sidebar, click **Subscription** (or navigate to your account settings).
2. The subscription page shows the available plans and pricing in your local currency.
3. Select **Monthly** or **Annual** billing — annual plans offer a discounted rate.
4. Click **Upgrade** on the plan you want.
5. You will be taken to a Stripe-hosted checkout page to complete payment.
6. Once payment is confirmed, your new plan activates immediately.

## Managing your subscription

To update payment details, cancel, or switch between monthly and annual billing:

1. On the Subscription page, click **Manage Billing**.
2. You will be taken to the Stripe customer portal where you can manage all billing settings.

## Pricing and currency

Prices are shown in your local currency based on the **country** set in your Account Settings:

| Country | Currency |
|---|---|
| Australia | AUD |
| New Zealand | NZD |
| United Kingdom | GBP |
| European countries | EUR |
| All other regions | USD |

If you have not set a country yet, an amber notice will appear on the subscription page. Go to **Account Settings** and set your country to see pricing in your local currency.

Your currency is fixed once your first payment is processed — this is a Stripe requirement and cannot be changed afterwards.
