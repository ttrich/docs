title: Navigating the Dashboard
description: An overview of the dashboard and how to find your way around CapraGene.
---

The dashboard is your home screen after signing in. It gives you a summary of your animals, active registrations, and recent activity.

## Key areas

| Area | What it shows |
|---|---|
| **My Animals** | A count of all animals in your herd |
| **Pending Registrations** | Registration applications currently awaiting review |
| **Recent Activity** | Latest updates across your records |
| **Quick Actions** | Shortcuts to add a new animal, start a registration, or browse the registry |

Use the **sidebar** on the left to navigate between sections of the system.

## Main sections

- **My Animals** — your full herd list with filters and search
- **My Registrations** — all your registration applications and their statuses
- **My Organisations** — organisations you belong to and the Membership Hub
- **Shows** — upcoming and past shows
- **Pedigree Registry** — the public registry browser
- **Admin** — platform administration (admin users only)
