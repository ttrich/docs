---
title: Completing Your Profile
description: Set up your name, photo, and bio so registrars and other breeders can identify you.
---

After signing in for the first time, complete your profile so other users and registrars can identify you.

## Steps

1. Click your name or avatar in the top-right corner of the screen.
2. Select **Profile** from the dropdown menu.
3. Fill in your **first name**, **last name**, **username**, and an optional **bio**.
4. Upload a **profile photo** by clicking the photo circle and selecting an image from your device.
5. Optionally add a **cover photo** for your profile page.
6. Click **Save** to apply your changes.

## Country & currency

Your **country** setting in Account Settings determines which currency is shown on the subscription page (AUD for Australia, NZD for New Zealand, GBP for the United Kingdom, EUR for European countries, and USD for all other regions).

To update your country:
1. Click your avatar → **Account Settings**.
2. Select your country from the dropdown.
3. Save. Pricing on the subscription page will update to reflect your local currency.
