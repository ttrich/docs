title: Notifications
description: How the notification bell and inbox work.
---

The bell icon in the top-right corner of every page shows your unread notifications.

## The notification bell

- A **red badge** shows how many unread notifications you have.
- Click the bell to open a **popover** with your most recent notifications.
- Click any notification to read the full details.
- Click **View all** to go to your full **Notifications Inbox** at `/notifications`.

## Notifications Inbox

The inbox lets you:
- Filter by **All**, **Unread**, **Announcements**, or **Reminders**
- Dismiss individual notifications
- Permanently delete notifications

## Pop-up reminders

Health, breeding, and birth reminders may also appear as pop-up alerts when they are due. Announcements from platform administrators are delivered to your bell, not as pop-ups.
