description: How to request access and sign up for CapraGene.
---

CapraGene uses an invitation-based onboarding model during its validation phase.

## Requesting access

1. Visit [capragene.com](https://capragene.com) and click **Request Access**.
2. Fill in the access request form with your name, email address, stud or organisation name, and your role (breeder, association, etc.).
3. Your request will be reviewed. Once approved, you will receive an email with a link to complete your signup.
4. Click the link in your email and follow the prompts. You can sign up with your email and password, or use **Google sign-in** for one-click access.

<Note>
  CapraGene currently onboards a limited group of breeders and organisations during its validation phase. Access is reviewed to ensure suitability for registry participation.
</Note>
