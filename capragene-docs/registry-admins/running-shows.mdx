---
title: Running Shows
description: Creating and managing shows through CapraGene as an organisation admin.
---

## Creating a show

1. From the organisation admin panel, click **Shows**.
2. Click **Create Show**.
3. Enter the show details:
   - Name, date, location
   - Entry open/close dates
4. Click **Save**.

## Adding sections and classes

Organise your show into sections (e.g., "Dairy Breeds") and classes within each section:

1. Open the show and click **Sections**.
2. Click **Add Section**.
3. Within a section, click **Add Class**.
4. For each class, configure:
   - Class name
   - Entry fee
   - Sex restriction (male / female / any)
   - Minimum and maximum age (months)
   - Breed restriction (optional)
   - Requires registry registration (yes/no)
   - Maximum entries allowed
5. Click **Save**.

## Reviewing and approving entries

1. Open the show admin panel.
2. Click **Entries**.
3. Review each submitted entry (animal, owner, class).
4. Click **Approve** or **Reject** each entry.

Approved entries confirm the animal's spot in the show. The owner is notified.

## Recording results

After the show:

1. Open the show admin panel.
2. Click **Results**.
3. For each class, enter:
   - Show class
   - Placing (e.g., 1st, 2nd)
   - Award (e.g., Champion, Reserve Champion)
   - Judge's notes
4. Click **Save**.

Results are visible to exhibitors on their animal's profile Shows tab.

## Financial overview

1. Open the show admin panel.
2. Click **Overview**.
3. The financial summary shows total entries and revenue collected, pending payments, and platform fees.

To receive entry fee payments, connect a **Stripe account** in your organisation settings.
