---
title: Reviewing & Approving Registrations
description: How to review pending applications and approve or reject them.
---

## Finding pending applications

1. From the sidebar, go to your organisation's admin panel.
2. Click the **Animals** tab (or **Pending Applications** tab).
3. All applications with status **Pending**, **DNA Required**, **DNA Submitted**, or **DNA Verified** will appear here.

Any duplicate detection flags (matching microchip or name across registries) will appear as yellow warning banners on the application.

## Reviewing an application

1. Click **Review** on any application.
2. The review panel shows:
   - Animal details (name, breed, sex, date of birth)
   - Owner information
   - Parent/pedigree information
   - Duplicate detection flags (if any)
   - DNA test status (if applicable)
   - Any attached documents
3. Review all details carefully.

## Approving a registration

1. In the review panel, confirm all details are correct.
2. Optionally enter or modify the **registration number** (or leave blank to auto-assign).
3. Click **Approve**.

The animal owner is notified by email. The registration number is assigned and appears on the animal's profile and pedigree.

<Note>
  If the registry requires DNA verification, the Approve button is disabled until the DNA status is **DNA Verified**.
</Note>

## Rejecting a registration

1. In the review panel, click **Reject**.
2. Enter a **reason for rejection** — this is sent to the breeder so they understand what to correct.
3. Click **Confirm Rejection**.

## Viewing registration certificates

Once approved, you can view the registration certificate from the Animals tab:

1. Find the approved animal.
2. Click **View Certificate**.
3. A printable certificate opens showing animal details, registration number, pedigree, and DNA tier.
