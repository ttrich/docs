---
title: Managing Members & Breeders
description: Managing who belongs to your organisation and their access levels.
---

## Inviting a member

1. From the sidebar, go to **Organisation Admin**.
2. Click the **Members** tab.
3. Click **Invite Member**.
4. Enter the member's **email address** and select their **role**:
   - Member
   - Registrar
   - Organisation Admin
5. Click **Send Invite**.

The invited user will receive an email. If they don't have a CapraGene account, they will be prompted to request access.

## Managing member roles

1. In the **Members** tab, find the member.
2. Click the role dropdown next to their name.
3. Select the new role.
4. Changes take effect immediately.

## Removing a member

1. In the **Members** tab, find the member.
2. Click the options menu (three dots) next to their name.
3. Click **Remove from Organisation**.
4. Confirm the action.

The member loses access to the organisation's registries and admin functions. Their personal animal records are not affected.
