## How transfers work

When a breeder initiates a transfer of a registered animal, the request is routed to the registry for authorisation. This ensures the registry maintains an accurate ownership record. All transfers are recorded in a full audit trail.

## Finding transfer requests

1. Go to your organisation's admin panel.
2. Click the **Transfers** tab.
3. All pending transfer requests appear here, showing:
   - The animal being transferred
   - Current owner (requestor)
   - Proposed new owner (recipient)
   - Date requested

## Approving a transfer

1. Click **Review** on the transfer request.
2. Verify the requestor and recipient details.
3. Click **Approve Transfer**.

The registry ownership is updated immediately. Both the current and new owner receive email notifications.

## Rejecting a transfer

1. Click **Review** on the transfer request.
2. Click **Reject**.
3. Enter a reason (visible to the requestor).
4. Click **Confirm**.
