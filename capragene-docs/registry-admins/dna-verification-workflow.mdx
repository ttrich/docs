---
title: DNA Verification Workflow
description: How to request, review, and verify DNA test documents as a registrar.
---

## Requesting a DNA test

When reviewing an application that requires DNA verification:

1. Open the review panel for the application.
2. In the **DNA Parentage Verification** section, click **Request DNA Test**.
3. The application status changes to **DNA Required**.
4. The owner is automatically notified by email and in-app.

## Reviewing a submitted DNA document

When an owner uploads their DNA document, the application moves to **DNA Submitted** and you will receive a notification.

1. Open the review panel.
2. In the DNA section, click the **PDF link** to open and review the document.
3. Check that:
   - The lab name matches an approved laboratory (if your registry has restrictions)
   - The animal ID and parentage match the application
   - The result is a pass

## Marking pass or fail

After reviewing the document:

- Click **Pass — DNA Verified** if the document confirms parentage. The status changes to **DNA Verified** and you can proceed to approve the registration.
- Click **Fail** if the document is invalid, from an unapproved lab, or shows a mismatch. The owner is notified and must resubmit.

## Approving after DNA verification

Once status is **DNA Verified**, the approval button becomes active. Follow the standard [approval steps](/registry-admins/reviewing-approving-registrations).
