## Viewing a pedigree

1. Open any animal's profile.
2. Click the **Pedigree** tab.
3. The interactive pedigree tree loads, showing multiple generations of ancestors.

## Understanding the tree

- The animal itself appears at the left (or centre).
- The **sire** (father) appears on the top branch; the **dam** (mother) on the lower branch.
- Each parent expands into their own sire and dam, and so on through the generations.
- Registered animals show their **registration number** on the tree.
- DNA-verified animals show a **verification tier badge**.

## Clicking on ancestors

Click any ancestor in the tree to open their profile (if they are in the system). This lets you trace bloodlines and verify registration status at any generation.
