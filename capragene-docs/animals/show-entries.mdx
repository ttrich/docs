## Finding a show

Shows are run by organisations. To find an open show:

1. From the sidebar, click **Shows**.
2. Browse the list of upcoming shows.
3. Click a show name to view the sections, classes, entry fees, and eligibility requirements.

## Entering a show

1. Open the show detail page.
2. Click **Enter Animals**.
3. Select the animal you want to enter.
4. Select the relevant **class** (classes may have age, sex, or breed restrictions).
5. If an entry fee applies, complete payment via the payment step.
6. Click **Submit Entry**.

Your entry will show as **Pending** until the show organiser approves it.

## Viewing show history and results

All show entries for your animals are visible on each animal's profile:

1. Open the animal's profile.
2. Click the **Shows** tab.
3. Results (class, placing, award, judge's notes) appear once the show organiser has recorded them.
