## Adding a new animal

1. From the sidebar, click **My Animals**.
2. Click the **Add Animal** button in the top right.
3. Fill in the animal details:
   - **Name** — the animal's registered or stud name (stored in uppercase)
   - **Date of Birth**
   - **Sex** — Male or Female
   - **Breed** — select from the available breed list
   - **Tag / ID number** — your farm or ear tag identifier
   - **Microchip number** (optional but strongly recommended for registry submissions)
   - **Colour / Markings** (optional)
   - **Production Type** — select one or more: Dairy, Meat, Fiber (controls which production tabs are visible on the profile)
4. Click **Save Animal**.

<Warning>
  Once an animal has an approved registry registration, the core identity fields (name, date of birth, breed, sex) are **locked** and cannot be edited. This protects the integrity of registered animals. Contact your registrar if a correction is needed after approval.
</Warning>

## Editing an animal

1. Open the animal's profile by clicking its name from **My Animals**.
2. Click the **Edit** button (pencil icon) at the top of the profile.
3. Update the relevant fields.
4. Click **Save**.

If the animal has an approved registry registration, a yellow **lock banner** will appear at the top of the edit form and the identity fields will be disabled.

## Uploading photos

Each animal can have multiple photos:

1. Open the animal's profile.
2. Click the **Photos** tab or the photo area at the top of the profile card.
3. Drag and drop images onto the upload area, or click to browse your device (JPG or PNG recommended).
4. To set a **cover photo**, hover over any uploaded photo and click **Set as Cover**.

## Animal status badges

Each animal card shows a status badge:

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Active | Currently in your herd |
| Sold / Transferred | Ownership has been transferred to another user |
| Deceased | Marked as deceased in the records |

## Production types

An animal can have more than one production type at the same time — for example, **Dairy + Fiber**. Setting a production type controls which tabs appear on the animal's profile:

| Production type selected | Tab shown |
|---|---|
| Dairy | Dairy Production |
| Meat | Growth & Output |
| Fiber | Fiber / Shearing |
