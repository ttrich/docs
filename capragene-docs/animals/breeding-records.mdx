title: Breeding & Reproductive Records
description: Recording matings, pregnancies, and offspring.
---

## Recording a mating

1. Open the **dam's** (female) animal profile.
2. Click the **Breeding** tab.
3. Click **Add Mating Record**.
4. Select or search for the **sire** (male).
5. Enter the **mating date** and mating method (natural, AI, ET).
6. If using AI from your semen inventory, select **Semen Source → From Inventory** and pick the straw lot. The straw count will automatically decrement.
7. Add any notes.
8. Click **Save**.

## Recording a birth / offspring

After a successful pregnancy, record the offspring:

1. Open the dam's profile.
2. In the **Breeding** tab, find the mating record.
3. Click **Add Offspring**.
4. Enter the offspring's details — or link to an existing animal already in the system.
5. The dam and sire will be automatically set as parents on the offspring's pedigree.

## Setting parents on an animal

You can also set an animal's parents directly from the animal's own profile:

1. Open the animal's profile.
2. Click **Set Parents** or the pedigree section.
3. Search for and select the sire and dam from animals already in the system.
4. Click **Save**.

<Note>
  Parent assignments are locked once the animal has an approved registry registration.
</Note>
