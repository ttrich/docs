---
title: Meat & Growth Records
description: Tracking weight records, growth milestones, and processing or sale records.
---

## Enabling growth tracking

The **Growth & Output** tab is visible on animals with **Meat** selected as a production type, or when your farm is configured as a Meat or Dual farm.

## Adding a weight record

1. Open the animal's profile and click the **Growth & Output** tab.
2. Click **Add Weight Record**.
3. Enter the date, weight (kg), and any notes.
4. Click **Save**.

A chart shows the animal's growth curve over time.

## Setting growth milestones

Milestones (e.g., weaning weight, 100-day weight) can be recorded separately:

1. In the **Growth & Output** tab, click **Add Milestone**.
2. Select the milestone type and enter the value.
3. Click **Save**.

## Recording processing or sale

1. In the **Growth & Output** tab, scroll to the **Processing / Sale** section.
2. Click **Add Record**.
3. Enter the date, type (Processing or Sale), weight, price per kg (optional), and notes.
4. Click **Save**.
