---
title: Transferring Animal Ownership
description: How to transfer a registered animal to a new owner.
---

## Initiating a transfer

1. Open the animal's profile.
2. Click **Transfer Ownership** (found in the actions menu or registrations section).
3. Enter the **email address** of the new owner.
4. Select which **registry registration** is being transferred (if applicable).
5. Add any notes.
6. Click **Send Transfer Request**.

## What happens next

- The transfer request is sent to the relevant registry for authorisation.
- Once the registry approves the transfer, the registration is reassigned to the new owner.
- The new owner receives an email notification.
- If the new owner does not yet have a CapraGene account, they will need to request access first.

## Checking transfer status

Go to **My Registrations** and find the relevant registration. Pending transfers show as **Transfer Pending** with the recipient's email address. You can cancel a pending transfer if needed.
