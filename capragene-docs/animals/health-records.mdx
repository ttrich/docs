## Adding a health record

1. Open the animal's profile.
2. Click the **Health** tab.
3. Click **Add Health Record**.
4. Select the record type:
   - Veterinary visit
   - Vaccination
   - Treatment / Medication
   - Condition / Diagnosis
   - General observation
5. Fill in the date, description, and any notes.
6. Optionally attach a document (e.g., a vet certificate PDF).
7. Click **Save**.

## Reviewing health history

The Health tab displays all records in reverse chronological order. You can:
- Filter by record type
- Click any record to expand the full details
- Download attached documents

## Setting health reminders

You can schedule reminders for upcoming health events (e.g., annual vaccinations):

1. On the Health tab, click **Add Reminder**.
2. Select the reminder type and date.
3. Click **Save**.

When the date arrives, a reminder notification will appear in your notification bell and may show as a pop-up alert.
