title: Fiber & Shearing Records
description: Tracking shearing events and fiber data for fiber-producing animals.
---

## Enabling fiber tracking

The fiber tab is visible on animals with **Fiber** selected as a production type.

To enable it:
1. Open the animal's profile and click **Edit**.
2. In the **Production Type** field, select **Fiber**.
3. Click **Save**.

The **Fiber** tab will appear on the animal's profile.

## Adding a shearing record

1. Open the animal's profile and click the **Fiber** tab.
2. Click **Add Shearing Record**.
3. Enter:
   - Shearing date
   - Fleece weight (kg)
   - Staple length (mm)
   - Micron count (if tested)
   - Any notes
4. Click **Save**.
