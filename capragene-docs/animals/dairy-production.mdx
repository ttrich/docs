---
title: Dairy Production Records
description: Recording lactation and milk production data for dairy animals.
---

## Enabling dairy tracking

To see dairy production tabs on an animal's profile, the animal must have **Dairy** selected as one of its production types:

1. Open the animal's profile and click **Edit**.
2. In the **Production Type** field, select **Dairy** (you can select multiple types).
3. Click **Save**.

The **Dairy Production** tab will appear on the animal's profile.

## Adding a lactation record

1. Open a female animal's profile.
2. Click the **Dairy Production** tab.
3. Click **Add Lactation**.
4. Enter the start date, end date (optional), and any notes.
5. Click **Save**.

## Recording milk yields

Within an active lactation, you can record individual milk tests:

1. Open the **Dairy Production** tab.
2. Find the lactation and click **Add Milk Record**.
3. Enter the date, morning yield (litres), evening yield (litres), and any quality notes.
4. Click **Save**.
